Invoice Excel Worksheet - Download

This worksheet tracks and validates invoices from Rider’s Depot, documenting received SKUs, pending quantities, extra items, and their corresponding values to ensure accurate inventory and payment reconciliation.

Step 1:Input, review and validate the invoiced documentation received from Rider’s Depot.

Step 2:Input, review and validate the items received from Rider’s Depot.

Step 3:Review the Output and Extra Items.

  • The Output columns display the SKUs that have been received, as well as the quantities still pending. They also show the value of each received SKU and the amount owed to the distributor.
  • The Extra Items columns display any unexpected products that have been delivered to our distribution center and received. This also includes the quantity and total cost of each product.

Step 4: Review the Quick View section for a summary of the invoiced items. This includes the quantity and value received, as well as any extra quantities and corresponding values.
